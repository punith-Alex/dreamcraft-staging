Bedroom Design Questions, Answered

How much does bedroom interior design cost in Chennai?
It depends on the wardrobe size, finishes, and scope — which is why we give you a transparent, itemized quote with every element listed: wardrobe, dresser, lighting, false ceiling, bed back panel. No hidden costs, no last-minute surprises. Book a free consultation and you'll have a clear number before you commit to anything.
Sliding, hinged, or walk-in wardrobe — which is right for my room?
It depends on your room's width and how you dress. Sliding shutters save floor space in compact rooms, hinged doors give full-view access and cost less, and walk-ins suit larger master bedrooms. We'll recommend the right type at your consultation — and show you all the options in 3D so you can compare before deciding.
My apartment bedroom is small. Can you still fit enough storage?
Yes — compact Chennai apartments are exactly what we plan for. Floor-to-ceiling wardrobes, lofts above, slim dressers, and bed-side niches recover every usable inch, while light tones and cove lighting keep the room feeling open. You'll see the exact fit in your 3D walkthrough before anything is built.
What warranty do you provide on wardrobes?
All our modular work — wardrobes included — carries a 10-year warranty, in writing. Combined with moisture-resistant materials chosen for Chennai's climate and soft-close hardware, your wardrobe is built to open smoothly for years to come.
