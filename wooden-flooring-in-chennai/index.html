We know the hesitation: "Will wood survive Chennai's humidity?" It's the first question every homeowner asks us — and the right one. The answer lies in the mechanism, not the marketing: engineered boards with stable cores, acclimatized on site before laying, moisture barriers underneath, and expansion gaps planned into every room. That's how a wooden floor stays flat, silent, and beautiful year after coastal year.

Wooden Flooring Questions, Answered

How much does wooden flooring cost in Chennai?
It depends on the wood type, area, and finish — which is why we give you a transparent, itemized quote instead of a vague per-sq-ft number. Materials, underlays, skirting, and installation are all listed line by line. No hidden costs, no last-minute surprises. Book a free consultation and you'll have a clear number before you commit to anything.
Will wooden flooring survive Chennai's humidity?
Yes — when it's specified and installed correctly. We recommend engineered wood with a stable core for most Chennai homes, acclimatize the boards on site before laying, install moisture barriers underneath, and leave calibrated expansion gaps. That combination is what keeps a floor flat and quiet through humid months.
How long does installation take?
Wooden flooring is one of the quicker transformations we do — most rooms are completed in days, not weeks, once the boards have acclimatized. You'll get a written timeline before we begin, backed by our 100% on-time guarantee.
How do I care for a wooden floor?
Gently — and not very often. A soft dry mop, prompt wiping of spills, felt pads under heavy furniture, and an occasional recommended cleaner is all it takes. We hand over a simple care guide with every floor so it ages gracefully for years.
