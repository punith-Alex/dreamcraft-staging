Modular Kitchen Questions, Answered

How much does a modular kitchen cost in Chennai?
It depends on size, layout, and finishes — which is why we give you a detailed, itemized quote instead of a vague per-sq-ft number. Every line is listed: materials, hardware, counters, installation. No hidden costs, no last-minute surprises. Book a free consultation and you'll have a clear number before you commit to anything.
Will I see the kitchen design before paying?
Yes — that's the heart of how we work. You'll get a full 3D visualization of your kitchen — colors, textures, and lighting — before execution begins, so you can make confident decisions with zero surprises.
What warranty do you provide on modular kitchens?
All our modular work carries a 10-year warranty, in writing. Combined with moisture-resistant materials chosen for Chennai's climate and our vetted vendor network, your kitchen is built to last well beyond the warranty card.
How long does a modular kitchen take?
Most kitchens are delivered within 4–6 weeks from design approval, on a written timeline with milestones you can track. We guarantee on-time delivery — it's one of the reasons Chennai homeowners choose us.
