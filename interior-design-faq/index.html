Your Questions, Answered Honestly

Hiring an interior designer is a big decision — and you deserve straight answers before you make it. Here's everything Chennai homeowners ask us, answered the way we'd want to be answered.

Book Free Consultation
Will the final outcome really match what was promised?
This is the fear we hear most — a gorgeous render, then a finished room that looks nothing like it. It's exactly why we built our process around 3D visualization before execution. You'll see your complete home on screen — every color, texture, and light — and refine it until it feels right. What you approve is what our team builds. No interpretation gaps, no surprises — just the home you already fell in love with on screen, now real.
How much does interior design cost in Chennai?
It depends on your home's size, scope, and finishes — which is why we'll never quote you a vague per-sq-ft number. Instead, you get a transparent, itemized quote: every material, fitting, and service listed line by line, so you know exactly where every rupee goes. And here's the part most people don't expect: thoughtful design often saves money, by preventing costly mistakes, impulse buys, and do-overs. Book a free consultation and you'll have a clear number before you commit to anything.
Will the budget go out of control?
We know the horror stories — a quote that quietly doubles by handover. That can't happen here, because your quote is itemized before work begins. No hidden costs, no last-minute additions. The price you approve is the price you pay, and if you request a change midway, it's priced transparently before it happens — so you stay calm and in control from start to finish.
Will the project get delayed?
Delays usually happen when no one owns the timeline. We do — in writing. You'll receive a written schedule with clear milestones at design approval, backed by our 100% on-time delivery guarantee. You can track every stage as it unfolds, so you always know exactly when you'll be walking into your finished home.
Can I trust Dreamcraft with my home?
Your home is personal, and handing it to strangers is a leap. Here's what that leap rests on: 100+ homes designed and delivered across Chennai over 15+ years, and a 4.9★ rating from 250+ reviews. Every project also gets a dedicated project manager — one accountable person you know by name, keeping your home on track from first sketch to final handover.
Will you understand my taste and practical needs?
We won't hand you a template — we'll listen first. Your project starts with a consultation where we hear your story: how your family cooks, hosts, prays, relaxes. Then, in our Design Your Story step, those details become vivid 3D visuals you refine with us until the design feels genuinely like you. If something doesn't feel right, we keep shaping it until it does.
Is professional interior design worth the investment?
Think of what unguided decisions cost: the sofa that doesn't fit, the finish that fades, the wall that gets redone twice. Professional design prevents those expensive do-overs. Our vetted vendor network gets things right the first time, and we steer you toward durable, timeless choices that won't need redoing in a few years. Guided decisions make your budget go further — and the result feel better, every single day you live in it.
Do I need to manage vendors myself?
Never. That vendor juggling — carpenter blaming electrician blaming painter — is exactly the chaos we exist to remove. Dreamcraft is fully turnkey: design, sourcing, carpentry, electrical, painting, and installation all handled by one team, under one dedicated project manager. You make one call when you have a question. That's it.
What happens after the project is done?
We don't disappear after handover. You'll stay connected with a team that continues to care — post-project support is part of every engagement, and all modular work carries a 10-year warranty, in writing. If a hinge needs adjusting or a drawer needs attention after you move in, the same team that built your home takes care of it.
How long does a full home take?
It depends on your home's size and scope — so instead of a vague promise, you get a clear milestone-by-milestone timeline at design approval, backed by our 100% on-time guarantee. You'll know your move-in date before work begins, and you'll watch each milestone tick past on schedule.
Do you work with my budget?
Yes — we plan budget-first. The design is built around what you're comfortable investing, never the other way around. We prioritize the high-impact areas — the spaces you see, touch, and live in most — so every rupee works harder. And the itemized quote keeps it all transparent from day one, so your budget stays yours.
Which areas of Chennai do you serve?
All of Chennai. From our studio at No 61, MMTC Colony, Nanganallur, we design homes across OMR, Velachery, Sholinganallur, Adyar, ECR, Thoraipakkam, Nungambakkam — and beyond. Wherever in Chennai you call home, we'll come to you for a free consultation and site measurement.
