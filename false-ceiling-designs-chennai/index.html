False Ceiling Questions, Answered

How much does a false ceiling cost in Chennai?
It depends on the material — gypsum, POP, or wood — and the design's complexity, which is why we give you a transparent, itemized quote instead of a vague per-sq-ft number. Every line is listed: framing, boards, lighting, finishing. No hidden costs, no last-minute surprises. Book a free consultation and you'll have a clear number before you commit to anything.
Gypsum, POP, or wooden rafters — which should I choose?
Each has its place. Gypsum gives clean, seamless finishes and quick installation — ideal for cove-lit living rooms and bedrooms. POP allows sculpted curves and custom detailing. Wooden rafters bring warmth and character to dining areas, stairwells, and feature spaces. We'll recommend the right choice for each room and show you exactly how it looks in 3D before you decide.
Will a false ceiling make my room feel lower or hotter?
Not when it's designed for the room. We plan drop heights around your ceiling height and AC placement, so rooms actually cool faster — a smaller air volume means your AC works less in Chennai's heat. Peripheral and layered designs keep the centre high, so the room feels open, never boxed in.
Can you add cove lighting to an existing ceiling design?
Yes — we design lighting into the ceiling from day one, but we can also retrofit coves, recessed spots, and pendant points into existing false ceilings. You'll preview the lighting mood in 3D first, so the evening glow you approve is the one you get.
