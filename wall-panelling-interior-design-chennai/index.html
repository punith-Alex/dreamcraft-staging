Wall Panelling Questions, Answered

How much does wall panelling cost in Chennai?
It depends on the wall size, panel type, and finish — which is why we give you a transparent, itemized quote instead of a vague per-sq-ft number. Panels, substrates, lighting, and installation are all listed line by line. No hidden costs, no last-minute surprises. Book a free consultation and you'll have a clear number before you commit to anything.
Will panelling hold up in Chennai's humidity?
Yes — when it's built on the right base. We use moisture-stable substrates and finishes chosen for Chennai's climate, with every joint and edge detailed and sealed. It's the same discipline we bring to all our woodwork, and it's why our walls stay crisp years after handover.
Can you panel a TV wall or pooja room specifically?
Absolutely — they're two of our most requested spaces. TV walls get concealed wiring, backlighting, and storage planned in from day one. Pooja rooms get panelling and carved detailing that honor tradition while sitting beautifully within a modern home.
Does wood slat panelling really improve acoustics?
It genuinely softens a room. Slats break up flat reflective surfaces, so echo is gently reduced — conversations feel warmer and TV sound feels cleaner. It won't replace a studio's acoustic treatment, but you'll notice the difference the first evening.
