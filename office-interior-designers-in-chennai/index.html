Office Interior Questions, Answered

How much does office interior design cost in Chennai?
It depends on the area, scope, and finishes — which is why we give you a transparent, itemized quote instead of a vague per-sq-ft number. Partitions, storage, feature walls, and installation are all listed line by line. No hidden costs, no last-minute surprises. Book a free consultation and you'll have a clear number before you commit to anything.
Do you take on all commercial projects?
We're primarily a residential design studio, so we take on office work as a select, limited offering — compact offices, clinics, and boutique workspaces where craftsmanship and detail matter. If that sounds like your space, we'd love to hear about it in a free consultation.
Will the work disrupt our business operations?
We plan around you. Execution follows a written timeline with clear milestones, work can be phased or scheduled outside business hours where needed, and our 100% on-time guarantee means your reopening date is a commitment, not an estimate.
Can the design reflect our brand identity?
That's the point of the first impression. Your brand colors, materials, and story shape the reception wall, signage niches, and lighting — and you'll review it all in 3D before work begins, so the space your clients walk into says exactly what you want it to.
